PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: December 7, 2025

Total Package Interview ("we," "us," or "our") operates totalpackageinterview.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Personal Information:

  • Email address (for account creation and communication)
  • Name (optional, for personalization)
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe; we do not store credit card details)

Usage Information:

  • Resume content (when you upload for analysis)
  • Interview answers (voice recordings and transcripts)
  • Usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Browser type, IP address, device information
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Process your transactions
  • Send service-related communications
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Respond to support requests
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

3. VOICE RECORDINGS

When you use our Interview Coach:

  • Voice recordings are processed in real-time by AI (OpenAI)
  • Recordings are converted to text for analysis
  • We do not permanently store voice recordings after processing
  • Transcripts may be temporarily stored to provide feedback
  • Voice data is not shared with third parties except our AI provider

4. DATA SHARING

We share your information only with:

Service Providers:

  • Stripe (payment processing)
  • OpenAI (AI processing for interviews and resume analysis)
  • Vercel (hosting infrastructure)
  • Email service providers (transactional emails)

We do not:

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share your data for marketing purposes
  • Provide your information to advertisers

Legal Requirements:

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety.

5. DATA SECURITY

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure payment processing through Stripe (PCI-compliant)
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. DATA RETENTION

We retain your information for as long as:

  • Your account is active
  • Needed to provide services
  • Required by law

You can request deletion of your account and data at any time by emailing support@totalpackageinterview.com.

7. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Export your data

To exercise these rights, email support@totalpackageinterview.com.

8. COOKIES

We use cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Improve our services

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

9. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Our Service uses third-party services with their own privacy policies:

  • Stripe: stripe.com/privacy
  • OpenAI: openai.com/privacy
  • Vercel: vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

10. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our Service is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover we have collected data from a child, we will delete it immediately.

11. INTERNATIONAL USERS

Your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from your country. By using our Service, you consent to this transfer.

12. CALIFORNIA RESIDENTS

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have additional rights:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale (we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising rights

Email support@totalpackageinterview.com to exercise these rights.

13. CHANGES TO PRIVACY POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email or through the Service. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

14. CONTACT US

Response time: Typically within 24 hours
